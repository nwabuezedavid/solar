The Organisation

RCI LTD (RC 7995135) is a leading renewable energy/solar and power company in Nigeria. We specialise in the design, procurement, construction, installation, and maintenance of solar power systems in Nigeria.

RCI Ltd was founded by Rosemary Benony, DR.Uche Iheanyi Uche, and Victor Nwatu in 2024 with the purpose of providing sustainable energy in the commercial and domestic market in Nigeria and West Africa.
